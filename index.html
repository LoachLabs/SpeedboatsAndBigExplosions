Who Are These Guys?

Juan Sanchez and The Bullet team up to form “Speedboats and Big Explosions”. These retro cops bring together the deep, funk and the tech to create a house music sound that is as awesome as their glorious moustaches.

They've been part of releases on Sonny Fodera's 'Beatdown' Label, Carl Hanaghan's 'Discopols Recordings', Midnite Sleaze's 'Bamboo Music', Jalapeno Records, Supermarket Unlimited, Emotive Sounds and System Recordings.

Their delicious Martini of funk, tech and soul is shaken, not stirred, and is always setting the right mood for the party.
